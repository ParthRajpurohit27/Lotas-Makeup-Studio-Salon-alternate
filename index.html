About Us

A Decade of Crafting Beauty Stories

Lotas Makeup Studio & Salon is Bhopal's most trusted ladies-only beauty destination, nestled in Sarvdharm B Sector, Kolar Road. For over 10 years, we have been the go-to sanctuary for women who seek the finest in beauty — from expert skin rituals and transformative hair care to breathtaking bridal artistry.

We believe every woman deserves to feel extraordinary — and we make that possible at prices that celebrate affordability. Our studio is equipped with premium, state-of-the-art machines, the finest professional products, and a fully air-conditioned, hygienic environment that makes every visit a luxurious experience.

Meet the Artist

The Expert Behind the Magic

Tanu
Founder & Lead Beauty Artist · Lotas Makeup Studio

With over a decade of hands-on expertise and formal training from some of India's most prestigious beauty institutions, Tanu brings an unmatched level of skill and artistry to every client she serves.

Having completed comprehensive courses in makeup, hair, skincare and facial treatments from the renowned Nikky Bawa institution, and having worked with elite establishments including Matrix Professional Salon and other leading studios, she carries a wealth of experience that is reflected in every brushstroke and every transformation.

Her philosophy is simple: every woman is beautiful — and her job is simply to help you see it too.
