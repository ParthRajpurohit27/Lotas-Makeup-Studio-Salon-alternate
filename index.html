About Us

A Decade of Crafting Beauty Stories

Lotas Makeup Studio & Salon is Bhopal's most trusted ladies-only beauty destination, nestled in Sarvdharm B Sector, Kolar Road. For over 10 years, we have been the go-to sanctuary for women seeking the finest in beauty — from expert skin rituals and transformative hair care to breathtaking bridal artistry.

We believe every woman deserves to feel extraordinary — and we make that possible at prices that are thoughtfully affordable. Our studio is equipped with premium, state-of-the-art machines, the finest professional products, and a fully air-conditioned, hygienic environment that makes every visit a luxurious experience.
