Our Journey

Born From a Passion for Beauty

Lotas Makeup Studio & Salon began as a dream — a vision to create a space where the women of Bhopal could experience world-class beauty services without compromise. Established over a decade ago in the heart of Kolar Road, we have grown from humble beginnings into one of Bhopal's most trusted and beloved ladies-only beauty studios.

Every year, thousands of women walk through our doors — brides on the most important day of their lives, professionals seeking a confident new look, and everyday women who simply deserve to feel extraordinary. We treat each client with the same care, the same artistry, and the same unwavering dedication.

At Lotas, beauty is not a luxury reserved for a few — it is every woman's right. That is why we offer premium services at prices that are thoughtfully accessible, without ever compromising on quality, hygiene or experience.

Meet the Founder

The Artist Behind Every Look

Tanu
Founder, Lead Makeup Artist & Beauty Expert · Lotas Makeup Studio & Salon

With a relentless passion for beauty and a meticulous eye for perfection, Tanu has built Lotas Makeup Studio & Salon into a name synonymous with trust, excellence and transformation in Bhopal.

Formally trained at the prestigious Nikky Bawa academy — one of India's most respected beauty institutions — Tanu has mastered the full spectrum of beauty arts: from advanced makeup techniques and skincare rituals to professional hair treatments and bridal artistry.

Her professional journey has taken her through some of India's finest beauty establishments including Matrix Professional Salon and other leading industry names. This diverse, high-level experience infuses every service at Lotas with a depth of knowledge that is truly exceptional.

Today, Tanu personally oversees every aspect of Lotas Makeup Studio — ensuring the highest standards in every service, every single day.
